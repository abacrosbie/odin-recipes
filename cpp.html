Cheesy Porky Pasta

Description

Absolute povo food I had back in uni.

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork chops
  • A bunch of pasta
  • fajita seasoning (schwartz is best)
  • as much cheddar as you can handle
  • a whole bunch of cream cheese, like half a POT
  • veggies are for the weak
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Set your pasta to cook in a hella salty pot of water, as salty as the atlantic as they say
  2. Chop up the pork chops
  3. Douse the pork in fajita seasoning, be careful too much and the intense flavour makes it SPICY, but don't be a wuss
  4. Cook those little porky pieces in a pan/pot/wok/vessel of choice in your favourite lubricant
  5. Admire the fond (the burnt bits if you over did it)
  6. Once the pasta has cooked, add to the pan and reserve some pasta water, we're not animals here
  7. Add the pasta water to the pot to finish cooking the pasta off
  8. Stir through both cheeses
  9. Bon appetite
