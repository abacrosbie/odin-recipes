Massaman Curry

Description

Beef massaman curry is a famous Thai beef stew that is the best of 2 worlds - it has the texture of stew with the flavor of curry. The sauce is intensely savory, thick, and luscious. Make sure you are using a good-quality curry paste (like Maesri) so you will get great depth of flavor without a lot of work.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • cornstarch
  • 2tbsp neutral oil
  • 6tbsp massaman paste
  • 1cup beef stock
  • can of coconut milk
  • 1lb pototes
  • 2tbsp water
  • 1 lime
  • 3cup rice
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • corriander
  • chilli pepper
